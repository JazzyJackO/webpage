ABOUT

Atari also known as Atari Corporation from 1984 to 1996, is a third-party company for Nintendo that was the leader of the video game market from 1975 to the early 1980s. They created quite a few systems including the 400, the 2600, the 5200, the 7800, Lynx, Jaguar and VCS.
